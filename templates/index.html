About Human Development Index

The Human Development Index (HDI) is a statistical measure developed by the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP). It evaluates countries based on health, education, and standard of living.

Health

Measured using Life Expectancy at Birth. A higher life expectancy generally indicates better healthcare and living conditions.

Education

Education is measured using Expected Years of Schooling and Mean Years of Schooling.

Income

Standard of living is measured using Gross National Income (GNI) per capita.
